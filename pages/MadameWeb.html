Madame Web

Cassandra Webb is a New York metropolis paramedic who begins to demonstrate signs of clairvoyance. Forced to challenge revelations about her past, she needs to safeguard three young women from a deadly adversary who wants them destroyed.

Director S.J. Clarkson
Writers Matt Sazama , Burk Sharpless , Claire Parker
Stars Dakota Johnson , Sydney Sweeney , Isabela Merced

In a switch from the typical genre, Madame Web tells the standalone origin story of one of Marvel publishing's most enigmatic heroines.
